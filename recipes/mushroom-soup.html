Mushroom Soup

Description

A flavourful and hearty mushroom-centric dish.

Ingredients

  • 30g unsalted butter
  • 1 onion
  • 2 garlic cloves
  • 400g white mushrooms
  • 200g brown mushrooms
  • 3 1/4 cups vegetable stock
  • 1/4 tsp salt
  • 1/8 tsp black pepper
  • 3/4 cup cream

Steps

  1. Chopping mushrooms: Cut mushrooms into 4 slices, then dice into 3 or 4 pieces
  2. Saute onion and garlic: Melt butter in a large pot over medium high heat. Add onion and garlic. Cook for 3 minutes until softened, but not golden.
  3. Cook mushrooms: Add mushroms and cook for 10 minutes, stirring regularly.
  4. Simmer 15 min: Add vegetable stock, salt, and pepper. Bring to a boil, then lower heat to medium and simmer for 15 minutes without a lid.
  5. Cream: Stir in cream, then simmer for 5 minutes.
  6. Blitz: Transfer to a blender (do in batches, if necessary).
  7. Return to pot: Simmer for a minute or two until bubbles caused by blending largely subside and soup is hot.
  8. Serve: Ladle into bowls.
