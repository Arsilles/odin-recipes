Chicken Satay Stir Fry

Description

Rich satay sauce with light peanut and coconut flavouring, stir fried with chicken.

Ingredients

  • 250g chicken breast

Satay Seasoning

  • 1/2 tsp coriander powder
  • 1/2 tsp cumin powder
  • 1/4 tsp turmeric powder
  • 1/4 tsp chilli powder
  • 1 1/2 tsp curry powder
  • 1/2 tsp salt
  • 1/4 tsp white pepper

Satay Sauce

  • 1 1/2 tbsp smooth peanut butter
  • 2 tsp brown sugar
  • 1tsp light soy sauce
  • 2 tsp rice vinegar
  • 2 tsp hot sauce
  • 1/2 tsp lemongrass paste
  • 1/3 cup coconut milk
  • 1/4 cup water

Stir Fry

  • 1 tbsp peanut oil
  • 1 onion, finely chopped
  • 2 garlic cloves, minced

Steps

  1. Cut chicken in half length wise then slice thinly.
  2. Rinse well, pat dry with paper towel then place in bowl.

Seasoning

  1. Mix seasoning together
  2. Sprinkle 1 tsp of seasoning over chicken. Mix to coat then set aside.

Sauce

  1. Place all ingredients except water in a bowl. Add remaining seasoning. Mix, then add water, and mix.

Stir Fry

  1. Heat oil in wok or skillet over high heat. Add onion and garlic, stir fry for 1 minute.
  2. Add chicken and stir fry for 2 minutes until just cooked through and a bit brown.
  3. Lower heat slightly, then add sauce. Stir fry until it reduces down and becomes thicker - 1 to 1 1/2 minutes.
  4. Serve immediately with rice.
